Physics-informed polarization geolocalization

A synthetic-data and hierarchical learning pipeline for predicting solar-elevation trajectories and regressing geolocation from polarization imagery.

  • Models solar-elevation dependencies instead of relying on generic augmentation.
  • Combines a Vision Transformer with a two-stage PointTransformer.

Intelligent Motion Lab · 2024

Robust 3D head pose estimation for visual servoing

A robotics perception pipeline sharpened by a Spring 2024 computer vision report on clinical head tracking, then extended toward multi-camera visual servoing.

  • Used 3DDFA_V2 with four selected landmarks and a UKF instead of relying on raw frame-to-frame detections.
  • Reported landmark tracking error under 5 pixels relative to 3DDFA_V2 on the BiWi Kinect dataset.
  • Exposed half-face poses as the hardest regime before integration into the broader UR5 visual servoing stack.

Sharif University of Technology · Journal paper

IMU-based telerehabilitation system design

The rehab thread now starts with the journal paper: a full system-design study for Parkinson's telerehabilitation that minimizes patient burden while preserving clinically useful recognition.

  • Optimized a 10-IMU setup down to a left thigh and left forearm pair.
  • Reached 95% recall on healthy-subject data and maintained above 80% after patient adaptation.
  • Structured the pipeline explicitly as data acquisition, optimal HAR design, and one-session PD adaptation.

Sharif University of Technology · Augmentation papers

Rehabilitation data augmentation studies

Two companion studies test augmentation from both sides: whether it improves HAR performance and whether the generated rehab motion still looks natural to experts.

  • Benchmarked six time-series augmentation methods from a 92% raw baseline up to 98% with jittering.
  • Used 6 IMUs, 21 therapeutic activities, and 18 expert raters to judge realism and variation.
  • Showed there is no one best augmentation method across all activity types, especially for dynamic walking tasks.
