Ongoing research · BioSensors Lab

Physics-informed polarization geolocalization

A synthetic-data and hierarchical learning pipeline for predicting solar-elevation trajectories and regressing geolocation from polarization imagery.

  • Models solar-elevation dependencies instead of relying on generic augmentation.
  • Combines a Vision Transformer with a two-stage PointTransformer.
  • Uses evaluation tied to strict sub-degree error thresholds.

SUNLAB · 2025

Multi-modal synthetic EHR with medical image generation

A conditional diffusion system for generating clinically meaningful images from structured patient visits and graph-based latent representations.

  • Multi-modal visit conditioning instead of isolated labels.
  • Graph neural networks for sparse clinical dependencies.
  • Designed around Digital Twin and External Control Arm use cases.

Intelligent Motion Lab · 2024

Robust 3D head pose estimation for visual servoing

A robotics perception pipeline that combines landmark detection, UKF filtering, and careful multi-camera calibration to produce a stable control signal.

  • Built for occlusion and extreme head angles.
  • Integrated into a shared UR5 visual servoing stack.
  • Validated on the RGB-D BiWi Kinect dataset.

Sharif University of Technology · 2019–2023

Wearable rehabilitation intelligence

A multi-paper research thread on inertial human activity recognition for Parkinson's disease and telerehabilitation.

  • Reduced a system to two sensors while preserving 95% recall.
  • Improved ConvLSTM accuracy from 92% to 98% with targeted augmentation.
  • Benchmarked multi-task CNN-BiLSTM models across 65 activities.
