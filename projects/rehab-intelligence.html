Paper 3

Qualitative validation of augmentation realism

Used six IMUs, 21 therapeutic activities, a 3D body model, and 18 expert raters to judge whether augmented signals still looked like real motion.

  • Time warping best preserved structure overall.
  • Amplitude warping helped specific activities like stairs and standing posture.
  • Walking remained the hardest regime to preserve convincingly.
